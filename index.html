Sihhathu - Your All-In-One Medical Solution

Sihhathu is an application under the theme of health and well-being, that we have designed to make health care services easier and more user friendly in the Maldives by integrating some basic features into one mobile application. Users will be able to gain access to these features on their smartphones anywhere in the Maldives but some features may require internet access and an additional device.
